About Booz'd Jamaica

Made in Jamaica.
Soaked in intention.

Booz'd Jamaica started with one idea: dessert should have a little danger in it. Not in a reckless way — in a deliberate, made-with-care, you-asked-for-this way.

Every cake is made to order. No pre-baked stock. No compromise on freshness. You order, you pay, we bake. That's it.

We use Wray & Nephew Overproof — Jamaica's most uncompromising spirit — in every single soak. You pick how deep you want to go. We handle the rest.
